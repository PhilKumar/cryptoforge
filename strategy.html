Start Campaign

Leave the time blank to auto-detect the mother candle from its high, on the timeframe picked above (5m looks back ~3.5 days).
5m — Initiate is a fresh campaign off a recent or minor MC, including a sub-mother marked inside a move already running; it stays 5m whatever chart you spotted it on. Older MC anchors deliberately to a bigger candle from the left. Either way the ladder decides escalation, not the kind: 5m, 15m and 1H all climb to the next rung as they outgrow the screen (5m → 15m → 1H → 4H), and 4H, 1D and 1W are fixed — 4H because it is the cap, 1D and 1W because they are off the ladder. A mother candle breaking above always restarts on 5m.
With a group budget set for a symbol, its campaigns share that one pot: a new campaign takes at most what running siblings have not committed, snapshotted at start. Set the budget to 0 to remove the group and go back to independent capital.
New campaigns start in paper mode by default. Flip to LIVE per campaign once you trust the behaviour — live entries rest on Binance as limit buys and the TP rests as a limit sell, so exits survive app downtime.
